Welcome to QuickFunds! These Terms and Conditions govern your use of our loan services in Kenya, Zambia, South Africa, Tanzania, Namibia, and Morocco. By applying for a loan, you agree to these terms.

1. Loan Eligibility

To qualify for a QuickFunds loan:

  • You must be at least 18 years old
  • You must be a resident of one of our operating countries
  • You must provide valid identification
  • You must have an active mobile money account (Airtel Money for most countries)

2. Loan Terms

  • Loan amounts range from 2,000 to 250,000 Kwacha (or local equivalent)
  • Loan periods: 1-2 years for loans up to 50,000; 3-5 years for larger loans
  • Annual interest rate: 15%
  • Repayment schedule: Annual installments

3. Collateral Policy

All QuickFunds loans require a refundable collateral deposit of 20% of the loan amount.

Your collateral will be:

  • Held securely in an escrow account during the loan term
  • Fully refundable upon successful loan repayment
  • Applied toward your final payment if you choose
  • Forfeited only in case of default after 90 days of non-payment

To claim your collateral refund after full repayment, contact customer service with your loan reference number.

4. Loan Application

By applying, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate personal and financial information
  • Authorize us to verify your information through third parties
  • Receive communication via SMS, email, or phone regarding your application

5. Disbursement

  • Approved loans are disbursed within 24 hours to your registered mobile money account
  • Disbursement times may vary based on mobile network providers
  • A 1% processing fee applies to all loans

6. Repayment

You agree to:

  • Make payments on or before the due dates
  • Pay a 5% late fee for payments over 7 days late
  • Communicate with us if you anticipate payment difficulties

7. Default Consequences

After 90 days of non-payment:

  • Your collateral will be applied to the outstanding balance
  • Your account will be referred to collections
  • Your credit score will be negatively impacted
  • Legal action may be pursued for recovery

8. Early Repayment

  • You may repay your loan early without penalty
  • Interest will be calculated only for the period the loan was active
  • Collateral will be refunded within 7 business days of early repayment
