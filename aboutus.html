About QuickFunds

Empowering financial freedom across Africa since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, QuickFunds began with a simple mission: to provide fast, accessible loans to individuals and small businesses across Africa. What started as a small operation in Nairobi has grown into a pan-African financial services provider, serving millions of customers in six countries.

We believe everyone deserves access to fair financial services, and we're proud to have disbursed over 5 million loans, helping our customers achieve their dreams.
