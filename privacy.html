QuickFunds Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2023

At QuickFunds, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our loan services in Kenya, Zambia, South Africa, Tanzania, Namibia, and Morocco.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Personal Identification: Full name, national ID, date of birth, photographs
  • Contact Information: Phone number, email address, physical address
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, mobile money numbers, credit history
  • Employment Information: Employer details, income level, pay slips
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process your loan application and determine eligibility
  • Disburse loans and collect repayments
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Improve our services and develop new products
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Communicate with you about your account

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Credit Bureaus: To report loan performance
  • Regulatory Authorities: As required by law
  • Service Providers: Such as payment processors and IT services
  • Business Partners: For approved marketing offers

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures including:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmissions
  • Secure servers with restricted access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Employee training on data protection

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information:

  • For active customers: Throughout your relationship with us
  • For loan applicants: 7 years after last application
  • For closed accounts: 7 years after account closure

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Lodge complaints with regulatory authorities

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Improve user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or SMS.
